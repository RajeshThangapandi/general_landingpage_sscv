# Landing Page Redesign - Chandigarh University Style

## Overview
The landing page has been redesigned to match Chandigarh University's UI/UX and layout structure while maintaining your original **blue (#0360d6) and gold (#ffc107) brand colors**.

## Key Changes Implemented

### 1. **Typography**
- ✅ Added **Poppins font** (weights 300-900) for modern, bold typography
- ✅ Updated all headings to use Poppins for a more contemporary look
- ✅ Maintained Lato and Lora as fallback fonts

### 2. **Hero Section - Dark Background**
- ✅ Changed hero background from white to **dark navy (#0B101E)**
- ✅ Updated all text colors to white/light colors for contrast
- ✅ Added **prominent ranking badge** displaying:
  - "TOP 5% UNIVERSITIES" in large gold text
  - NIRF ranking information
  - Professional dark card design with gold accents
- ✅ Stats icons now have gold-tinted backgrounds
- ✅ Contact info icons changed to gold color

### 3. **Button Styles**
- ✅ Changed all buttons from pill-shaped (50px radius) to **rectangular with 8px border-radius**
- ✅ Maintained blue background color for all CTA buttons
- ✅ Kept consistent hover effects and shadows

### 4. **Section Backgrounds**
- ✅ Trust section: Changed to **light beige (#F9F5F2)** background
- ✅ Stats section: Kept dark gradient background
- ✅ Alternating white and beige backgrounds for visual hierarchy

### 5. **Sticky Bottom Banner**
- ✅ Added **scholarship banner** at bottom of page
- ✅ Golden gradient background (#ffc107 to #daa520)
- ✅ Blue "APPLY NOW" button
- ✅ Displays: "Grab up to 100% Scholarship Worth ₹200 Cr"

### 6. **Design Elements Matching CU**
- ✅ Large, bold typography throughout
- ✅ Spacious layouts with generous padding (80-100px vertical)

- ✅ Professional color scheme alternation
