## üé® **PHOTOGRAPHY GUIDELINES**

### **Technical Requirements:**
- **Resolution:** Minimum 1920x1080, prefer 4K
- **Orientation:** Both landscape and portrait
- **Lighting:** Natural light preferred, golden hour ideal
- **Focus:** Sharp, professional quality
- **Background:** Clean, uncluttered
- **Colors:** Vibrant but natural

### **Composition Tips:**
- Rule of thirds
- Leading lines (pathways, corridors)
- Depth of field (blurred backgrounds)
- Eye-level and varied angles
- Include university branding where possible

### **Editing Style:**
- Bright and vibrant
- Slightly increased saturation
- Professional color grading
- Consistent look across all photos
- Minimal filters, natural appearance

---

## üïê **BEST TIMES TO SHOOT**

**Golden Hour (Best Light):**
- Morning: 6:30 AM - 8:30 AM
- Evening: 4:30 PM - 6:30 PM

**Avoid:**
- Harsh midday sun (11 AM - 2 PM)
- Overcast/rainy days (unless intentional)
- Empty campus (shoot when students present)

**Best Days:**
- Weekdays when classes are in session
- During events/activities
- Avoid exam periods (stressed students)
